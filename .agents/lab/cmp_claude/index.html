第一性原理

First · Principles · Thinking

把任何问题拆到无法再质疑的事实，再从那里向上重建。 Boil a problem down to the truths you cannot doubt — then build your answer up from there, owing nothing to how it was done before.
1

定义 · What it is

从根本，
而非从惯例。From the root, not the routine

第一性原理，是把一件事物追溯到它最基础、不可再分解的真相，然后只用这些真相重新推导结论。

Most of our reasoning runs by analogy: we copy what already exists and tweak it a little. It's fast and usually safe — but it quietly inherits every assumption baked into the original, including the wrong ones.

First-principles thinking does the opposite. You discard the inherited answer, strip the problem down to physics, math and verified fact, and ask: given only what is certainly true, what is actually possible?

“The first basis from which a thing is known.”

— Aristotle, on πρῶτον (the first principle) · 亚里士多德
类比 → 复制已有 第一性原理 → 从零重建 问： 这真的必然成立吗？

实例 · Worked example

电池组的成本The price of a battery pack

A classic case (Elon Musk, c. 2008): everyone “knew” battery packs were hopelessly expensive — until someone refused to reason by analogy. 同一个问题，两种推理，得出完全不同的结论。

推理方式 A · By analogy

类比推理It's always been this way

  1. 电池组历来卖到 ~$600/kWh。
    Packs have always cost ~$600/kWh.
  2. 过去贵 → 未来也会贵。
    Expensive before, so expensive forever.
  3. ∴ 电动车永远做不便宜。
    Therefore EVs can't be cheap.
$600/ kWh
assumed floor

材料只占售价的 ~13%。剩下的 ~$520/kWh ≈ 87% 不是物理定律，而是制造、规模、设计与利润——全部都是可压缩的。If physics only demands $80, the other 87% is an engineering problem, not a law of nature. 这正是第一性原理打开的空间。

方法 · How to do it

五步，落到地面。A five-step method

01

定义问题Define

说清你真正要达成的目标，剥掉任何已假设好的解法。State the goal — not a solution in disguise.

02

拆到底Decompose

不断追问“这必然为真吗？”，直到只剩物理定律与已验证的事实。Keep asking until only facts remain.

03

质疑假设Challenge

标记每一个“大家都这么做”。继承下来的，不等于正确的。Inherited ≠ true.

04

重新构建Rebuild

只用这些基本事实，向上推导出方案——无视别人怎么做。Reason upward from the fundamentals.

05

验证迭代Test

拿现实来检验，再循环。现实是唯一的裁判。Measure against reality; iterate.
